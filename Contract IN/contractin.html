About Us

Our company, Contract IN, aims at providing the perfect relationship between employees and their employers. We also prioritize the safety and rights of employees, ensuring compliance with labor laws. Our company was formed on 19th October 2023.

Our mission is to alleviate poverty in our country by creating job opportunities through digital media, a feat that was previously not possible. We provide a wide range of job opportunities for both skilled and unskilled labor, as well as a diverse set of options for employers.

The privacy of employees and employers is our utmost priority. Any information shared between employers and employees is strictly confidential. Our values are what we strive to achieve.

- Team Contract IN
