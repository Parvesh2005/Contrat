Questions and Answers

Q. What is the difference between unskilled and skilled labour?
Ans. Skilled labor requires specialized training whereas unskilled labor does not.
Q. What are the advantages of employing unskilled workers?
Ans. Unskilled labor is good enough for the task at hand and is the least expensive option. From the workers's perspective, sometimes this is the highest paying job available to them.
Q. How many unskilled workers are there in India?
Ans. In India out of 487 million workers, not even more than 5% of the workers have received any formal skills training.
